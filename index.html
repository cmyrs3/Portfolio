A little bit about me

My name is Carrissa and I am a full-time freelance web designer whose passion lies in creating dynamic and beautiful web pages.

My journey into tech started with a daring leap away from my corporate job into a new career field which I had absolutley no proior experience with.
I jumped in with both feet and began taking classes online with skillcrush.com. Through taking the “Break Into Tech” course paired with a lot of research, and trial & error… I was able to kickstart my career with the tech industry!

When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I had found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I’ve made a full swith to front-end development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

I live in Michigan with my family of four who has lovingly supported me on my journey! Without their help, I wouldn’t be where I am today!
